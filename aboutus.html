NUGEN COMMUNICATIONS SDN BHD was incorporated since May, 2017. We spealize in the provision of Engineering, Procurement, Construction and Commissioning (EPCC) Services for Telecommunication, Building Works and Information Communication Technology (ICT) Infrastructures throughout Sarawak.
