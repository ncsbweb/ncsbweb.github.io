About Us

NUGEN COMMUNICATIONS SDN BHD was incorporated on the 25th of May, 2017. We specialize in the provision of Engineering, Procurement, Construction and Commissionning (EPCC) Services for Telecommunication Infrastructures throughout Sarawak.

We are a dynamic company who are still growing and always ready to learn and improve ourselves by exploring new solutions and technology that can improve the livelihood of our society.

NUGEN COMMUNICATIONS SDN BHD is a 100% Bumiputera, Sarawak owned company With registered Trade License under TELECOMMUNICATIONS WORKS.
